About Us

Riding with GoRider is an easy, affordable, and super-fun way to explore NYC. Just hop on an ebike or classic ride—and be on your way.

At GoRider, we are passionate about revolutionizing the way people move through cities.

2008: Founding in the Big Apple

GoRider was founded in the bustling city of New York in 2010 by a group of avid cyclists and urban mobility enthusiasts. Fueled by their passion for sustainable transportation and their love for the vibrant city, they set out to revolutionize commuting in the Big Apple.

2010: Launch of the First Bike Sharing Program

GoRider launched its inaugural bike sharing program in select neighborhoods of New York. With an initial fleet of 500 bicycles and strategically placed docking stations, GoRider aimed to provide New Yorkers with an alternative mode of transportation that was efficient, eco-friendly, and convenient.

2013: Integration of Electric Bikes

Recognizing the demand for faster and more efficient transportation options, GoRider introduced electric bikes to its fleet in 2016. The addition of electric bikes provided riders with an effortless way to navigate New York's streets, especially during longer commutes or hilly terrain.

The primary motive of GoRider is to revolutionize urban transportation and promote sustainable mobility through our bike sharing system. Our aim is to make commuting in the city easier, more efficient, and enjoyable for everyone. We understand the challenges of navigating through congested streets and limited parking options, which is why we provide a network of conveniently located docking stations and a diverse fleet of bikes. By offering this flexible and accessible transportation option, we empower individuals to choose an eco-friendly alternative to traditional vehicles, reducing carbon emissions and alleviating traffic congestion. Furthermore, we strongly believe in promoting healthier lifestyles. Cycling is not only a sustainable mode of transportation, but it also offers numerous health benefits, such as improved cardiovascular fitness and reduced stress levels. We strive to create a city where people can experience the joy and freedom of cycling while contributing to a greener and more sustainable environment. With GoRider, we are committed to redefining urban mobility and making a positive impact on the lives of individuals and the communities we serve.From our diverse fleet of bicycles to our user-friendly technology, GoRider is committed to enhancing urban mobility while reducing carbon emissions and promoting healthier lifestyles. Join us on this exciting journey as we transform the way you navigate the Big Apple, one pedal at a time.

Sustainable Mobility Award

GoRider was honored with the Sustainable Mobility Award in 2022 for our significant contribution to sustainable transportation solutions. This prestigious award recognizes our dedication to reducing carbon emissions, alleviating traffic congestion, and promoting greener cities through our bike sharing system.

City Choice Award for Best Bike Sharing System

Voted by the residents and visitors of New York, GoRider received the City Choice Award for Best Bike Sharing System. This award reflects the positive impact GoRider has made in the community, providing convenient and eco-friendly transportation options to enhance the quality of urban life.

Innovator of the Year

GoRider was recognized as the Innovator of the Year by the Urban Mobility Association for our cutting-edge technology, user-friendly mobile app, and commitment to continuously enhancing the bike sharing experience. This award underscores our dedication to leveraging innovation for the benefit of our riders.
